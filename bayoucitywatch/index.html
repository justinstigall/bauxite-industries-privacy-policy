About

Know what's happening in Houston right now

Bayou City Watch pulls active incident data directly from the City of Houston and pins every active call on a smooth, interactive map. Whether you heard sirens on your street or want to stay aware of your neighborhood, the information you need is a glance away.

Live Police Incidents

Precise Geocoding

Auto-refresh
