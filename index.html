Privacy Policy

Bauxite Industries — Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

Bauxite Industries ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information you may encounter while using our applications and services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Our applications do not require account creation, login, or any form of user registration.

Tracking and Analytics

We do not use any tracking technologies, including but not limited to cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, device fingerprinting, or third-party analytics services. We do not monitor or record your usage of our applications.

Data Storage

No user data is stored on our servers or on any third-party infrastructure controlled by us. Any data processed by our applications remains solely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Our applications may use open-source map data and tile services. We do not control those services and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies. We do not pass any personally identifiable information to these services.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal information from any users, we collect none from children either.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our applications after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our public repository or contact us through the applicable app store listing.
